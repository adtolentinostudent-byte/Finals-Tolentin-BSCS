Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror series created by the Duffer Brothers. Set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, the show follows a group of kids who encounter mysterious government experiments, supernatural forces, and a strange girl with telekinetic powers known as Eleven.
